About AutoMax Sales

Driving excellence since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by automotive enthusiast Michael Rodriguez, AutoMax Sales began as a small family-owned dealership with a passion for quality vehicles and exceptional customer service. What started as a single lot with just 15 pre-owned cars has grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive retailers.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include new vehicle sales, comprehensive financing options, and a state-of-the-art service center. Our commitment to integrity, transparency, and customer satisfaction has remained unwavering throughout our growth.

Today, AutoMax Sales employs over 50 dedicated professionals and serves thousands of customers annually. We take pride in our reputation for honesty, fair pricing, and personalized service that treats every customer like family.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional automotive solutions that exceed customer expectations through quality vehicles, competitive pricing, and unparalleled service while building lasting relationships based on trust and integrity.

Our Vision

To be the premier automotive destination recognized for innovation, community involvement, and setting industry standards for customer satisfaction and ethical business practices.

Community Involvement

At AutoMax Sales, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us throughout the years. We actively participate in local events, sponsor youth sports teams, and support charitable organizations.

Our annual "Wheels for Charity" event has raised over $100,000 for local food banks, and we regularly donate vehicles to deserving families in need. We're proud to be not just a business, but a contributing member of our community.
